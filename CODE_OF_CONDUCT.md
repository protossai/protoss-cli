# Code of conduct

We want participation in this project to be rigorous, candid, and safe.

## Expected behavior

- Critique ideas, specifications, and evidence without attacking people.
- State assumptions and conflicts of interest.
- Make room for contributors with different levels of standards and domain experience.
- Treat security, privacy, accessibility, and harmful-use concerns as technical requirements.
- Preserve disagreement accurately; do not manufacture consensus.
- Respect confidential information and do not post customer or employer data without permission.

## Unacceptable behavior

- harassment, threats, discrimination, or sexualized conduct;
- deliberate intimidation or sustained personal disruption;
- publishing another person's private information;
- bad-faith manipulation of reviews or governance;
- knowingly submitting material without the right to license it; or
- using project channels to expose credentials, private packs, or security vulnerabilities.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the project maintainers through the organization contact
mechanism. Maintainers may warn, moderate, temporarily restrict, or permanently remove a participant
when necessary to protect the community.

Enforcement decisions should be documented internally, handled consistently, and avoid unnecessary
disclosure about reporters or affected people.
